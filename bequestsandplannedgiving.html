Bequests and Planned Giving

After more than three decades in the business of training top journalists working in countries with a challenged democracy, we know that our efforts continue to be needed. Please consider remembering Alfred Friendly in your will, and help ensure the future for a global free press.

With a bequest to Alfred Friendly Press Partners, you can:

  • Honor a family member, friend or journalist whose work you admire
  • Endow a Fellowship
  • Sponsor an Annual Fellow

We encourage you to discuss your options with your attorney or estate planner, or contact us for arranging a discussion with our experts in Planned Giving.

Some of your options include making:

  • A cash bequest: You designate a specific amount in your will.
  • Residuary bequest: Any part of your estate — all or a percentage of the remainder after the payment of other bequests and expenses – can be committed to Alfred Friendly Press Partners.
  • Retirement plan gift: Designate Alfred Friendly Press Partners as a beneficiary of your retirement plan.

Sample Will language:

“I give, devise and bequeath to Alfred Friendly Press Partners, a Missouri nonprofit corporation and a qualified 501 (c) (3) organization, located in Columbia, MO, Federal Tax ID Number 52-1307387, the sum of __________ ( % of my estate), (or other personal property herein described) to be used for its general programs in journalism as it shall determine.”

Contact us with questions or if you require additional information.

About Us

Alfred Friendly Press Partners provides training in the United States and abroad for journalists from countries with underdeveloped media. During the six-month fellowship program, reporters train at the Missouri School of Journalism and within leading U.S. newsrooms so they can practice journalism that is ethical, innovative and influential. Fellows learn from mentors and editors with broad experience.
